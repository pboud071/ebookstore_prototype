OUT OF CONTROL

by Del Mundo Rob
Description
Out of Control: Football's Worst Offenders examines more than 50 players past and present who have become famous for heinous crimes away from the gridiron as well as great catches, tosses and tackles on it. It cannot explain why heroes and superstar athletes knowingly risk fame and fortune to commit these acts, but it can document and describe the criminal behaviour which is so prevalent.
